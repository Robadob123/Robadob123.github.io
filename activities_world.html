Do you like traveling to new exciting places? Well if you do, would I highly recommend you traveling to These amazing places shown down below!

1. Denmark skiing without ice, on a Building!

In Copenhagen there is a massive waste plant, which turns human waste into energy by burning it. On top of the narrow building there is a big artificial skiing reservation. It is one of the biggest ski resorts in the world.

2. Norway's Love there Trolls

In Ringedalsvatnet, across Skjeggedal and around Tyssedal you will find one of the most amazing sites in Norway. It is called the Trolltongue and is a big rocky cliff on top of a mountain.

3. Icelands steamy spa

In the beautiful country of Island, Reykjavik you will win big glazers, hot steamy geisers, volcanoes and much wildlife. But it wouldn’t be an Iceland if you won't relax and enjoy a spa from hot steamy water from natural heat coming from geysers and volcanoes. It may be expensive, but certainly worth the money.

4. Grand Canyon Rafting

Whether hurtling downstream or navigating treacherous rapids that threaten to overturn your dingy, river rafting is an exhilarating way to get your pulse racing and your clothes soaking. Grab a paddle, hop in an inflatable raft and explore some of the world's most daringly turbulent waters.

5. Cave climbing in Norway

The 45-meter ascent at Hanshelleren cave in Flatanger, a three-hour drive north of the city of Trondheim, is considered to be the world’s hardest single rope-length climb It is one of the biggest caves in the world, and many professional climbers have tried to climb it, including Magnus Midtbø.
