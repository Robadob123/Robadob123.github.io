2. Cricket

Cricket is a popular sport in England, Pakistan, and of course INDIA! Even though cricket was invented in south-east England in the late 16th century. Today cricket has more than 220 million players, and is most enjoyed by young cricket players.

3. Table Tennis

The game which has the same rules as normal tennis, but is in a smaller sized game on a table. The sport is most popular in the paralympics and China. You can find at least 5 tennis players at every street corner in a big city in China. Table tennis have more than 300 million players worldwide, and is topped number #7 on the world's greatest sports.
