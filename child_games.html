Games you played as a child, or games you should play no mather the age.

1. Tag

Tag is a beloved and easy game around the whole world. You might remember the game when your siblings, or other family members came around and played a fun and a little competitive game with each other.

2. Red light, Green light

I probably don’t even need to introduce the game to you because you probably remember seeing it on the popular KoreanTV series on Netflix called “Squid Game”. But for all the boomers who haven't seen the serie, or even the game, here is the explanation. Red light, green light is a game where someone is standing between a wall, and shouts Red light, green light. The other people have to run to the wall, but when the “leader” shouts red light, then you have to stand as still as you can. When the “leader” shouts green light, the other people can run to try to be the next “leader”.

3. Hide and Seek

Very easy game, but still fun. Okay, so begin with a seeker. A seeker is one who counts up to a given number, for example up to 30 sec. The other players have to hide, and the one who first gets found has to be the next seeker, and the last people to be found wins the game.

4. Bomb tag

Bomb tag is a Norwegian favourite, and is about tossing a ball to each other. But, if you drop the ball a timer from 10 sec starts ticking, and you have to pass the ball as fast as you can before it “explodes”. When the ball “explodes” and you're the person holding the ball, well then you're simply out.

5. Cube Games

The cube game is a little complicated to explain in text, so instead there is a video down below which you can follow. But anyways the game itself is very fun, and you can find the game at a low price on the internet or retail stores.

6. Marbles

You probably have played many different games using marbles such as trying to hit them with other marbles, trying to tosh them in a hole (that’s what she said), or clinking it with each other.

7. Chess

Ches is more of a strategy game, rather than a physical game. You probably played it with somebody else randomly just to relax and use your brain. Today cheez is a world class mastery sport, and is played by many people.

8. Hangman

This game is excessively popular if you have a piece of paper, and a pen. So the game works by a stickman almost getting dead, and you as the player have to save him by guessing a letter, and eventually it becomes a word.

9. Hot potato

Easy game, still very fun. Imagine you holding the hottest piece of potato in the world, but the “potato” could be any kind of object (For example a ball). You can only carry it in 1 sec and throw it immediately to somebody else. If the “potato” touches the ground, then you're out of the game.

10. Jump Rope

This game is well known all over the world. Because of the simplicity, many people have made it hard, and even a sport. You may have played the game in your local neighborhood. The rules of the game are easy: jump directly over a long rope without touching it. Two other people have to hold the tip of the rope, and swing it over the “jumper's” head.
