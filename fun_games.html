From the creators of Hitman 1 and 2, made the sequel of the new hitman 3. You can play the game on PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, Switch and Stadia. The level design is more complex, and more fun. The controllers feel almost exactly like Hitman 2. You can even play the game in VR, and the game only costs $50 dollars on epic games.

3. FIFA 22

The new FIFA game is the first developed football game exclusively made for the PS5 to show how capable the console really is. The game has the same gameplay as the other previous FIFA games, but has better graphics, more characters to unlock and a bigger story mode. The full game cost $100 dollars, but you can get a cheaper price online.
