Need money fast? Well why not try working in some small jobs, or clean your neighbours window. And there are more tips than that if you read the article shown down below.

1. Get a small job

If you are around 13 to 15 years of age would it be perfect to get a job. Even though you can't get a bigger job, and higher pay than working in a real shop. But you can get a smaller job, by helping your neighbors do any kind of cleanup, sell newspapers, help small businesses to lift something, and you can even get a job for planting trees (depending on where you live around your area).

2. Get paid for driving people

You can use the app Uber to drive people, and get paid quickly. You do need a driving license and a car, but if you do then get paid with Uber. The problem is that if you use a car running on gas, then you have to spend extra money from the money you earned on gasoline. But if you have an electric car, such as a Tesla or a BMW. Can you get free charging spots without spending a single dollar (depends on the area you live in).

3. Rent your house

You can get paid by renting your house on the app Airbnb. As Uber instead of renting your own car to drive people, you can rent your house. You can choose how much the person using Airbnb is paying you to stay in your home. But be careful, it’s strangers living in your home for a period of time, and you don’t know if he or she has done something bad. But Airbnb is a great app, and you should try if you want to earn quick and easy cash. But it’s of course your choice if you want strangers living in your house, maybe they're nice.

4. Sell items on the internet

If you have something laying around your home, and don’t have any need for it, it would be smart to sell it to somebody else who may need for example your old chair. Different websites such as Ebay, Amazon, Wish, and the list goes on, let’s you sell items which you could earn money from. Just remember to sell your items at a reasonable price.

5. Try pawning bottles

If you live in for example Norway, Germany, Sweden, or some states in the U.S such as Hawaii, Iowa, Maine, Massachusetts, Michigan and New York, then you're lucky. Because there are machines collecting plastic bottles which you have collected, and in exchange you can get up to 0,2 or 0,3 dollars per bottle. This does depend on where you live, but often grocery stores have one of these pawn machines. The plastic bottles do also get recycled, so you can make a decent amount of money and in the same be green.
